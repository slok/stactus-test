Resolved - Jul 30, 16:22

On July 30th, 2024, between 12:15 UTC and 14:22 UTC the Codespaces service was degraded in the UK South and West Europe regions. During this time, approximately 75% of attempts to create or resume Codespaces in these regions were failing.

We mitigated the incident by resolving networking stability issues in these regions.

We are working to improve network resiliency to reduce our time to detection and mitigation of issues like this one in the future.

Investigating - Jul 30, 16:16
