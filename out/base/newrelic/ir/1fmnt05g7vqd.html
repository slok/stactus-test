Resolved - Jan 21, 09:00

Between 01:00 UTC and 08:00 UTC on January 21st, some customers in the US and EU regions may have experienced missing or delayed infra-metrics for Azure integrations as well as delayed or missing alert notifications for related data.

To prevent our customers from receiving a high volume of false positive alert notifications, notifications for affected data were temporarily suppressed during this time.
