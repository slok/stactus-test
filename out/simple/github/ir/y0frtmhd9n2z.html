Incident with Codespaces


Incident resolved in 9m33.926s

Resolved

On June 27th, 2024, between 22:38 UTC and 23:44 UTC some Codespaces customers in the West US region were unable to create and/or resume their Codespaces. This was due to a configuration change that affected customers with a large number of Codespace secrets defined.

We mitigated the incident by reverting the change.

We are working to improve monitoring and testing processes to reduce our time to detection and mitigation of issues like this one in the future.
