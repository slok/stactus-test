Alerts UI Irregularities


Incident resolved in 7h32m4.991s

Resolved

Between 13:00 UTC on March 6th and 20:00 UTC on March 7th, some customers in the US region may have intermittently experienced out-of-date views for dashboards and UIs which rely on entity tags. Existing alert notifications were not impacted; however, some customers may have been unable to edit or view alert conditions related to entity tags.

We have investigated and resolved this interruption and the above services have returned to normal operation.
