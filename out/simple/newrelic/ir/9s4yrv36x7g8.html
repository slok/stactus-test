Data Ingest Irregularities in EU and US Regions


Incident resolved in 10m16.081s

Resolved

Between 16:05 UTC and 16:47 UTC on November 8th, some customers in the EU region as well as a small percentage of US customers may have experienced errors when sending data to New Relic, issues in viewing data in real time, and delayed or missing alert notifications related to real time data.

We have investigated and resolved this interruption and the above services have returned to normal operation.
